Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Template notice: adapt this policy to your own setup (analytics, form provider, jurisdiction) before publishing.

The short version

This is a personal portfolio. It sets no cookies, runs no advertising, and collects nothing about you unless you deliberately send it — for example, through the contact form. It is designed to work with the minimum possible data.

What this site stores in your browser

One value: your light/dark theme choice, kept in your browser's localStorage under the key theme. It never leaves your device, and clearing your browser data removes it. If you install the site as an app, your browser also caches pages for offline use — again, entirely on your device.

The contact form

If you submit the contact form, the name, email address and message you type are delivered to me so that I can reply. Depending on configuration, delivery happens either through your own email application (nothing passes through a third party) or through a form-delivery service, in which case that provider processes the submission on my behalf under its own privacy terms. I keep messages only as long as our correspondence needs and never sell or share them.

Analytics

If analytics are enabled, this site uses a privacy-focused, cookie-free service that records aggregate page views only — no personal identifiers, no cross-site tracking, no fingerprinting. If analytics are not enabled, nothing is measured at all.

Hosting logs

Like almost every website, the hosting provider that serves these pages may keep short-lived technical logs (IP address, requested page, timestamp) for security and reliability. These are governed by the host's own policy and are not used by me to identify visitors.

External links

Links to GitHub, LinkedIn and other external sites lead to services with their own privacy practices, which I don't control.

Your rights

If you've emailed me and would like your correspondence deleted, ask and it will be. For anything privacy-related, contact hello@aarav.dev.example.

Changes

If this policy changes, the date above changes with it. Material changes will be noted here plainly rather than buried.
